turu

High Lulani

とる

/ˈtuɽʉ/

transitive transformative verb

to close; to fill

Example

Suma turu gurrisu.

。すま とる ぐっりす。

suma turu gurrisu.

1tra close door.

“I closed the door.”

Derived Terms

turulu とるを to open up

Antonym
