tali

High Lulani

たえ

/ˈtʰalɨ/

transitive motive dative verb

to take away from someone

Example

ʔaggami pi kalutuhhisa tali ’ita’i.

。あっがみ ・ ぴ ・ かをとっひさ ・ たえ ・ いたい。

’aggami pi kalu-tuhhisa tali ’ita’i.

Policeōfficer pst;sta abl-farmer take car.

“The police officer took the car from the farmer.”

mathematics

subtract; minus

Example

Qihha tali bila ’anu kifi.

。ねっは ・ たえ ・ びわ ・ あぬ ・ きべ。

qihha tali bila ’anu kifi.

Zero take ree equal three.

“Zero subtract minus three equals three.”
