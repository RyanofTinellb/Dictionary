-ini

High Lulani

、いに

/ɨnɨ/

derivational cisclass suffix (noun / verb)

diminutive; derives a term of lesser intensity or size from another term; dim

Notes

Obscene when added to a noun referring to a person.

Examples

’ahatini あはてに contentment

tissini てっせに breeze

ranini らにに chips

bi’ini びいに to itch
