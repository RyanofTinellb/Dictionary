High Lulani

り

/ɽɨ/

derivational suffix (noun: abstract → animate)

derives an animate noun from an abstract noun

Examples

’axasiri あざせり adult

’asilari あせわり friend

dissuri でっすり stranger

ʔirri’ari いっりあり person from Irìa

gufu’iri ぐふいり radio operator

derivational suffix (noun: abstract → inanimate)

derives instruments from an abstract noun

Examples

gufu’iri ぐふいり radio

cahuri やほり market stall

derivational suffix (noun)

other than

badiri ばでり world

liqari えげり body
