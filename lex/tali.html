tali

High Lulani

たえ

/ˈtʰalɨ/

transitive motive dative verb

to take away from someone

Examples

ʔaggami pi kalutuhhisa tali

’ita’i.

。あっがみ ぴ かをとっひさ たえ いたい。

’aggami pi kalu-tuhhisa tali ’ita’i.

Police_officer pst;sta abl-farmer take car.

“The police officer took the car from the farmer.”

subtract; minus
