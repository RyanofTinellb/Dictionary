-ba

High Lulani

ば

/bə/

derivational suffix (verb → noun)

derives an animate agentive noun from a verb

Examples

  • ’ussaba うっさば follower
  • caba やば helper
  • hisuba ひすば administrator

derivational suffix (number → inanimate noun)

polyhedron; three-dimensional shape

Derived Terms

  • kifiba きべば tetrahedron
  • nuruba ぬるば cube
  • kifibara きべばら octahedron
  • guhiba ぐひば dodecahedron
  • takkiba たっきば icosahedron
  • pu’iba ぷいば sphere
