’i

See also: -’i

High Lulani

い

/ʔi/

ambitransitive communicative verb

to say; to speak; to tell

Negative Complement

sajja さっぢ silence

Derived Terms

    • ’isinna いせっな to tell a story
    • ’i’issi いいっせ to sing
    • ’ibibu いびぶ to complain
    • ’idajabi いだぢび to read aloud
    • ’idata いだた to reply
    • ’icipu いちぷ to ask
    • ’ikahu いかほ to grunt
    • ’iqaca いげや to give one’s point of view
    • ’ilagi いわぎ to scream
    • ’iluxxa いをっざ to request
    • ’ifuhhanaku いふっはなく to agree
    • ’ihucu いほゆ to inform
    • daru’i だるい because
    • gufu’i ぐふい radio

Examples

ʔusu jusi ’i li lu’i fu ra’u.

。うす よせ い え をい ふ らう 。

’usu jusi ’i li fu ra’u.

1int 3ani;dat say com love 2tra prs;sta.

“I told her that I love you.”

ʔusu palu’i ’i.

。うす ぱをい い。

’usu pa-lu’i ’i.

1int towards-love speak.

“I talked about love.”
