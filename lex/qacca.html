Random Entry

qacca

High Lulani

げっや

/ˈŋacːə/

ambitransitive perceptive verb

choose; decide; prefer; rather

Derived Terms

qacca’a げっやあ choice

Example Sentences

ʔusu qacca ’iti.

。うす ・ げっや ・ いて。

’usu qacca ’iti.

1int choose milk.

lit. “I choose milk.”

“I’ll have the milk.”

ʔusu ku’iti qacca.

。うす ・ くいて ・ げっや。

’usu ku-’iti qacca.

1int dat-milk choose

lit. “I choose towards milk.”

“I prefer milk.”
