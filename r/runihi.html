runihi

High Lulani

wNH

/ɾʉ.ˈnih.ɨ/

intransitive stem verb
to be similar to
Thematic Relations

theme subject

stimulus dative, haru, ’adi

Examples

ʔusu ba’u runihi.

.uz / bu / wNH.

1int 2abl same.

“I’m you.”

ʔusu ’adifilli runihi.

.;uz / aDF;L / wNH.

1int close-2int same.

“I’m similar to you.”
