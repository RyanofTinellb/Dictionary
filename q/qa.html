qa

High Lulani

(q)

/ŋə/

derivational suffix (numeral, verb, pronoun → noun)
nominaliser; nmn
Definition

Denotes a specific case of a general number, verb or pronoun

Examples

paguqa (p<q) mine

baqa (bq) yours

disiqa (DSq) theirs
