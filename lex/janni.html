janni

High Lulani

ぢっに

/ˈɟaɲːɨ/

intransitive kinetic verb

to accelerate; to move around; to vibrate

Derived Terms

    • janni’ita’i ぢっにいたい to drive
    • janni’issa ぢっにいさ to vibrate
    • jannimufa ぢっにむへ to dance
    • jann·nika ぢっににか to travel
    • jannililu ぢっにえを to take the stairs
    • jannihusabi ぢっにほさび to gesture
    • janni’u’ili ぢっにういえ to slide
    • jannibitta ぢっにびった to leap
    • jannisufi ぢっにすべ to jump
    • jannimaʔu ぢっにまっう to ascend
    • jannulu ぢっぬを to decelerate
