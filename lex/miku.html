miku

High Lulani

みく

/ˈmikʉ/

intransitive adjectival chromatic verb

to be red; to make red

Example

ʔarapahi cani miku.

。あらぱひ やに みく。

’ara:-pahi cani miku.

face-1gen prs;dyn red.

“I blushed.”

Derived Terms

mikumulu みくむを to be crimson

piba miku ぴば みく deep red
