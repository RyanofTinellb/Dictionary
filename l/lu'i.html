lu’i

High Lulani

(Wi)

/ˈluʔ.ɨ/

transitive stem verb
love
Thematic Relations

experiencer subject

stimulus object

Example

ʔusu lu’i fu.

(.uz / Wi / v.)

1int love 2tra.

“I love you.”

Derived Terms

lu’ipu’a (Wioa) lover

lu’iki (WiK) soul

lu’atinna (WaT;n) lovingly

lu’ica’i (Wici) to romantically love

lu’isamma (Wici) to unconditionally love

abstract stem noun
love
Notes

any compound based on the verb lu’i can also be used as a noun
