ga

High Lulani

が

/ga/

intransitive adjectival chromatic verb

to be black; to turn black

Example

ʔusu gakka si’apa.

。うす がっか せあぱ。

’usu ga-kka si’apa.

1int black-abp meal.

“I burnt the meal.”
