tana

High Lulani

tn

/ˈtʰan.ə/

abstract noun

there is; what there is

Example

Notes

while this is not a noun in the strict sense of the word, it does act like one when used by itself as a subject. This noun can be used as the base to nominalise adpositions.

Examples

tanadi tnD what is above

tanajimuli tnJUL what is outside

See Also
