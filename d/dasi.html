dasi

High Lulani

(dS)

/ˈdas.ɨ/

transitive stem verb
to place in the mouth; to eat; to chew; to suck; to lick; to drink.
Thematic Relations

agent subject

patient object

Examples

ʔusu dasi da.

(.uz / dS / d.)

1int eat water.

“I drank water.”

piba dasi to eat the most

ninadasiru for eating

Derived Terms
