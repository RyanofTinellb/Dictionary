Welcome to the Tinellbian Languages Dictionary!

About

This dictionary aims to describe all the vocabulary items of the twenty Tinellbian languages, consisting of High Lulani and its descendants. In addition, miscellaneous English neologisms have been added, including proper nouns.

The dictionary forms a background source for The Coelacanth Quartet, in conjunction with the Grammar Guide.
