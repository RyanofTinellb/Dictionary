nufira

High Lulani

ぬべら

/nʉfiɽə/

modal adverb

optative; want

Notes

Only used in first person, or second person questions.

Example Sentences

ʔusu ra’u dihuri nufira.

。うす ・ らう ・ でほり ・ ぬべら。

ʔusu ra’u di-huri nufira.

1int prs;sta down-sea want.

“I want to be under the sea.”

Derived Terms
