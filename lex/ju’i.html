ju’i

High Lulani

よい

/ˈɟuʔɨ/

quasi-transitive intransitive symmetric verb

link; join; wear

Derived Terms

ju’isatadu よいさたど to meet

’usaju’i うさよい to find

mathematics

plus

Example

Kifi ju’i nuru ’anu salumi.

。きべ よい ぬる あぬ さをみ。

kifi ju’i nuru ’anu salumi.

Three add four equal seven.

“Three plus four equals seven.”
