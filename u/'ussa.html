’ussa

High Lulani

u;s

/ˈʔus.sə/

transitive stem verb
to follow; to obey
Definition
  1. to move behind in the same direction or path
  2. to do as ordered

Thematic Relations

agent subject

goal dative

recipient object

Examples

ʔusu ba’u ’ussa.
