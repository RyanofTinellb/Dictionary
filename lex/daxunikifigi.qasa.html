daxunikifigi qasa

High Lulani

だずにきべぎ・げさ

/dəˌɕunɨˈkʰifɨgiˈŋasə/

derived animate compound noun animal

coelacanth; three-tail fish

Etymology

daxuni だずに tail + kifi きべ three + gi ぎ to have

qasa げさ fish

Notes

Named for its three-lobed caudal fin
