sida

High Lulani

(Sd)

/ˈsid.ə/

inanimate stem noun
heart (physical organ)
Usage Notes

refers to the aspects of the heart dealing with the heartbeat, as a notification of physical or emotional stress, et cetera. Cf: mulukku (UW;>).
