dumi

High Lulani

どみ

/ˈdumɨ/

abstract noun

nighttime hours; night; evening

Antonym

musa むさ daylight

abstract noun

attempt; try to

Notes

Does not imply anything about success or failure.

When used with ablative promotion, gives nuance of encouraged.

Example

Quhu kupaji dasikka mihu dumi.

。もほ ・ くぱじ ・ だせっか ・ みほ ・ どみ。

quhu

ku-paji dasi-kka mihu dumi.

3tra dat-fruit eat-abp 3int try.
