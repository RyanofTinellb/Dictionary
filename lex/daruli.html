daruli

High Lulani

だるえ

/dəˈɽulɨ/

inanimate derived noun

wrong way

Definition

  1. A road that does not lead to an expected destination.
  2. A one-way street, if travelled in the incorrect direction.

Etymology

daru だる road + -li え not
