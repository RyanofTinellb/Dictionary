da’aru

High Lulani

(daw)

/də.ˈʔaɹ.ʉ/

modal stem adverb
it exists; it happened; experience; it could happen
Examples

ʔusu qixa lu’i da’aru.

(.uz / Qx / Wi / daw.)

1int pst;dyn love exist.

“I have fallen in love before.”

ʔusu labu lu’i da’aru.

(.uz / lO / Wi / daw.)

1int fut;dyn love exist.

“I could fall in love.”
