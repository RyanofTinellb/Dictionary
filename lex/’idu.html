’idu

High Lulani

いど

/ˈʔidʉ/

transitive transformative verb

to create; to make

Definition

bring something into physical form from materials

Examples

ʔusu haru’u’a ’idu ’ita’i.

。うす はるうあ いどいたい。

’usu haru-’u’a ’idu ’ita’i.

1int with-wood make car.

“I built a car from wood.”
