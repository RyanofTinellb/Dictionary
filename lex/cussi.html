cussi

High Lulani

ゆっせ

/ˈcʰusːɨ/

ambitransitive perceptive verb

feel; touch

Example

ʔusu ra’u kunacca cussi.

。うす らう くなっや ゆっせ。

’usu ra’u ku-nacca cussi.

1int prs;sta dat-clothing touch

“I feel the clothing.”

ʔusu ra’u cussi nacca.

。うす らう ゆっせ なっや。

’usu ra’u cussi nacca.

1int prs;sta touch clothing.

“I touch the clothing.”

Derived Terms

cussi’ala ゆっせあわ to be hot

cussidicci ゆっせでっち to be cold
