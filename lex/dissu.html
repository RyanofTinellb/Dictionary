Random Entry

dissu

High Lulani

でっす

/ˈdisːʉ/

ambitransitive perceptive verb

to feel

Notes

Used for emotions and loves, often compounded. Such compounds tend to be adjectival verbs.

Example

ʔusu sacu dissu ’ahati.

。うす さゆ でっす あはて。

1int 2abl feel happiness.

’usu sacu dissu ’ahati.

“You make me happy.”

Derived Terms
