-ci

High Lulani

ち

/ci/ · /cɨ/

derivational nominalisation suffix (verb → noun)

creates an abstract noun from an adjectival or chromatic verb

Notes

This suffix is used to derive the names of colours from their respective verbs

Derived Terms

kiʔaci きっあち white

namaci なまち weakness
