ma

High Lulani

(m)

/mə/

clausal conjunction
where; when
Notes

most often used as a prefix for adverbial location clauses

Example

ʔusu dasi ma’adi, nara.

(.uz / dS / maD , nr.)

1int eat where-near, sleep.

I eat near where I sleep.

derivational suffix (adverb)
terminative, used for adverbs.

/ma/

transitive stem verb
to think with the soul
Derived Terms

ma’iludihha (miWD;h) to be considerate

mafatti (mf;T) to decide to

marasi (mrS) to want
