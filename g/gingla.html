gingla

English

/ˈgiŋ.glə/

abstract stem noun
planes of existence; ghostly realms
Definition

There are four ginglaic dimensions. These correspond to the seven planes in some esoteric systems, or to otherworldly realms, i.e.: in order to explain paranormal events.

Notes

Xaraduluri are capable of moving through gingla.

Translations

High Lulani: maʔu m;u
