cura’a

High Lulani

ゆらあ

/ˈcʰuɽəʔa/

derived noun

time

Definition

a duration of time in which to complete a task

Notes

poetic

Example

Cura’a ra’u pixi nufira.

。ゆらあ らう ぴし ぬべら。

cura’a ra’u pixi nufira

pass-nmn prs;sta 1dat wish.

“I wish I had time.”

Etymology

cura ゆら pass + ’a あ nmn
