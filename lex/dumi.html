dumi

High Lulani

どみ

/ˈdumɨ/

abstract noun

nighttime hours; night; evening

Antonym

musa むさ daylight

abstract noun

attempt; try to

Notes

Does not imply anything about success or failure.

When used with ablative promotion, gives nuance of encouraged.

Example

Quhu kupaji dasikka mihu dumi.

。もほ くぱじ だせっか みほ どみ。

quhu ku-paji dasi-kka mihu dumi.

3ani;tra dat-fruit eat-abv 3ani;int try.

“She encouraged him to eat fruit.”
