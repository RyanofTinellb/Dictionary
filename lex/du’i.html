Random Entry

du’i

High Lulani

どい

/ˈduʔɨ/

adposition

beyond; opposite

Notes

tends to mean ‘beyond’ when the area can be seen, ‘opposite’ otherwise.

Examples

du’imusa どいむさ away from the sun

du’icica どいちや on the other side of the fence
