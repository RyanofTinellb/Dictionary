ju’i

High Lulani

Yi

/ˈɟuʔ.ɨ/

ambitransitive stem verb
link; join; wear
Thematic Relations

patient, theme subject, object, dative, haru

Example

Suma ju’i sunu.

.zm / Yi / zI.

1tra link cloak.

“I put my cloak on.”
