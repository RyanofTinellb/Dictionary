cussi

High Lulani

(y;S)

/ˈcʰus.sɨ/

ambitransitive stem verb
feel; touch
Thematic Relations

agent, experiencer subject

stimulus dative

theme object

Example

ʔusu ra’u kunacca cussi.

(.uz / ru / >n;c / y;S.)

1int prs;sta dat-clothing touch

“I feel the clothing.”

ʔusu ra’u cussi nacca.

(.uz / ru / y;S / n;c.)

1int prs;sta touch clothing.

“I touch the clothing.”

Derived Terms

cussi’ala (y;Sal) to be hot

cussidicci (y;SD;C) to be cold
