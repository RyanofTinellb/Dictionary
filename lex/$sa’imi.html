Sa’imi

High Lulani

さいみ

/saˈʔimɨ/

proper derived noun

Caemi; goddess of light, energy and chaos; light-giver

Notes

Also the name given to the personification of the Nuobi sun.

Etymology

sa’i さい light + -mi み giver
