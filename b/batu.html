batu

High Lulani

(be)

/ˈbatʰ.ʉ/

inanimate stem noun
collection; group; store
Definition

a group of objects collected together, or a collected mass

See Also

nuhubatu (IVbe) book

qapi (qP) chain

ju’ipu’a (Yioa) assembly

’uxxi (u;X) crowd

stem mathematics
multiplied by
Example

Kifi batu nuru ’anu sabilla

(.KF / be / Iw / aI / sB;l.)

three multiple four equal one-ree.

“Three times four is twelve.”

See Also

’anu (aI) equal

ju’i (Yi) plus

tali (tL) minus

saki (sK) divide
