Random Entry

tiku

High Lulani

てく

/ˈtʰikʰʉ/

intransitive kinetic verb

turn; rotate

Definition

To rotate on one’s own axis.

Derived Terms

tikutiqaxi てくてげし to tilt

adposition

around

Example

tikunarra てくなっら around the corner

tikulinu てくえぬ around the tree
