xu

High Lulani

ず

/ɕu/

transitive apparent verb

to put out; to radiate; to broadcast

Notes

Inanimate themes are objects, animate themes use ka

Examples

Filli pixi xusina.

。べっえ ・ ぴし ・ ずせな。

filli pixi xu-sina.

2int 1dat seem-beauty.

“You seem pretty to me.”

Filli ka’aggami xu.

。べっえ ・ かあっがみず。

filli ka-’aggami xu.

2int as-policeman.

“You look like a policeman.”

Notes

Used for physical themes. Cf: xi し seem
