dissu

High Lulani

でっす

/ˈdisːʉ/

ambitransitive perceptive verb

to feel

Notes

Used for emotions and loves, often compounded. Such compounds tend to be 。うす ・ さゆ ・ でっす ・ あはて。

1int 2abl feel happiness.

’usu

sacu dissu ’ahati.

“You make me happy.”
