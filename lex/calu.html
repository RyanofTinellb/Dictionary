calu

High Lulani

やを

/ˈcalʉ/

abstract noun

dimension

Definition

  1. One of ten directions in which something can be measured
  2. One of four ways in which something can be measured

Derived Terms

caluduku やをどく the set of dimensions

Hypernyms

bitta びった time

sufi すべ space

maʔu まっう gingla

mathematics

exponential; power

Example
