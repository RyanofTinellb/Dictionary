cikki

High Lulani

(C;K)

/ˈcʰik.kɨ/

ambitransitive stem verb
to hear telepathically; to see empathically
Thematic Relations

experiencer subject

stimulus dative

theme object

direction pa

Examples

ʔusu ba’u cikki.

(.uz / bu / C;K.)

1int 2dat hear.

“I heard you telepathically.”

ʔusu cikki fu.

(.uz / C;K / v.)

1int hear 2tra.

“I listened to you empathically.”

ʔusu pafilli cikki.

(.;uz / pF;L / C;K.)

1int towards-2int hear.

“I telepathically listened in your direction.”
