xiku racaxa laru ru qapi

High Lulani

しくらやざわるるげぴ

/ˌɕikʉ ɽaˈcaɕə ˌlaɽʉ ɽu ˈqapʰɨ/

idiomatic inanimate noun

method for doing something

Examples

piba kuppu xiku racaxa disi ’usaju’illuru lanu qapi

ぴばくっぷしくらやざいせうさよいっをるわぬげぴ

piba kuppu xiku raca -xa disi ’usaju’i -illu -ru lanu qapi

most strong com hang-dtv 3ani;int find-psv-ger fut;dyn rope

lit: “the strongest rope off which to hang finding him”

“The best way to find him”
