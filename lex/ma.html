ma

High Lulani

ま

/mə/

clausal conjunction

where; when

Notes

most often used as a prefix for adverbial location clauses

Examples

ʔusu dasi ma’adi, nara.

。うすだせまあで、なら。

’usu dasi ma-’adi, nara.

1int eat where-near, sleep.

“I eat near where I sleep.”

/ma/

ambitransitive communicative verb

to think with the soul

Derived Terms

ma’iludihha まいをでっは to be considerate

mafatti まへって to decide to

marasi まらせ to want
