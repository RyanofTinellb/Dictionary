dasi

High Lulani

だせ

/ˈdasɨ/

transitive transformative verb

to consume; to eat; to chew; to suck; to lick; to drink

Examples

xu’a dasi da.

。ずあ だせ だ。

xu’a dasi da.

bird consume water.

“The bird is drinking water.”
