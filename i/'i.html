’i

High Lulani

(i)

/ʔi/

transitive stem verb
to say; to speak; to tell
Derived Terms

’isinna (iS;n) to tell a story

’icipu (iCo) to ask

’i’issi (ii;S) to sing

’ibibu (iBO) to complain

’idajabi (idjB) to read aloud

’ifuhhanaku (iv;hn>) to agree

’iqaca (iqc) to give one’s point of view

’ikahu (ikV) to grunt

’ilagi (ilG) to scream

Thematic Relations

agent subject

experiencer dative, object

topic pa

speech object

Aspectual Meanings

dynamic to say

stative to give a speech

iterative to chat

gnomic to be talkative

Examples

ʔusu jusi ’i li lu’i fu ra’u.

(.uz / YS / i / L / Wi / v / ru .)

1int 3dat say com love 2tra prs;sta.

“I told her that I love you.”

ʔusu palu’i ’i.

(.uz / pWi / i.)

1int towards-love speak.

“I talked about love.”
