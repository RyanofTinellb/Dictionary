-ini

High Lulani

(/iN)

/ɨ.nɨ/

derivational suffix (noun)
diminutive; derives a term of lesser intensity or size from another term
Usage Notes

Obscene when added to a noun referring to a person.

Example

’ahatini (ahTN) contentment

tissini (T;SN) breeze

qikannini (Qk;NN) puppy

ranini (rNN) chips

bi’ini (BiN) to itch
