tsarship

English

/ˈtsaːʃɪpʰ/

inanimate noun

divine vessel

Definition

A vessel piloted by gods or other divine forces, able to move through time, space, reality and gingla.

Etymology

tsar + ship

Translations

High Lulani: xaraduluri ざらどをり
