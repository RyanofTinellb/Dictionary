High Lulani

さ

/sə/

derivational suffix (noun: abstract → animate)

derives an animate noun from an abstract noun

Examples

pucasa ぷやさ optimist

sasa ささ introvert

dattusa だっとさ god

diss·sa でっすさ youth

mulisa むえさ fool

luxirasa をしらさ twin

tuhhisa とっひさ farmer
