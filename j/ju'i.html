ju’i

High Lulani

(Yi)

/ˈɟuʔ.ɨ/

ambitransitive stem verb
link; join; wear
Thematic Relations

patient, theme subject, object, dative, haru

Example

Suma ju’i sunu.

(.zm / Yi / zI.)

1tra link cloak.

“I put my cloak on.”

Derived Terms

ju’ipu’a (Yioa) assembly

ju’ulu (YuW) to undress

ju’iduku (YiE>) marry

ju’isatadu (YistE) to meet

’usaju’i (usYi) to find

mathematics
plus
Example

Kifi ju’i nuru ’anu salumi.

(.KF / Yi / Iw / aI / sWM.)

Three add four equal seven.

“Three plus four equals seven.”
