tana

High Lulani

たな

/ˈtʰanə/

abstract noun

there is; what there is

Example

Tana ru lulani.

。たな る をわに。

tana ru lulani.

there_is prs;gno queen.

“There is a queen (in existence).”

Notes

while this is not a noun in the strict sense of the word, it does act like one when used by itself as a subject. This noun can be used as the base to nominalise adpositions.

Examples

tanadi たなで what is above

tanajimuli たなじむえ what is outside
