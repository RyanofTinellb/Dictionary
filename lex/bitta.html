Random Entry

bitta

High Lulani

びった

/ˈbitːə/

inanimate noun

time

Definition

the two temporal dimensions

Notes

The first dimension of time is subjective: a person’s birth is before their death.

The second dimension is objective: if one time-travels into the past, then one’s death may well be upstream of one’s birth.

Derived Terms

jannibitta ぢっにびった to time travel

Hypernym
